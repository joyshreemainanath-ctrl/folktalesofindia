About This Project

Folktales of India is a reading-focused archive dedicated to preserving and sharing the rich oral traditions of Indian folklore.

Project Vision

This website aims to create an accessible, beautifully presented collection of Indian folktales, myths, and legends. Each story is presented in a clean, long-form layout designed for focused reading and cultural exploration.

How to Explore

You can browse our collection in several ways:

  • By Story Index - Browse all available tales
  • By State & Region - Explore stories organized by their geographical origins
  • Featured Tales - Discover highlighted stories from our collection

The Collection

Our archive includes folktales, myths, and legends from across India, with a focus on preserving the cultural context and regional significance of each story. As this project grows, we continue to add more tales and regions.
