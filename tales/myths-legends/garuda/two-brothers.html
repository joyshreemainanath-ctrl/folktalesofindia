The Tale of Two Brothers

Reading time: 4 to 5 min

Cycle: Garuda (The King of Birds) Source: Tale of Astika - Adi Parva, Mahabharata Theme: Curse, Hunger, Fate

An ancient feud between an ascetic elephant and tortoise provides Garuda with a mighty feast.

Two brothers once turned their greed into a terrible curse, taking birth as an elephant and a tortoise locked in endless conflict.

When Garuda sought sustenance for his vast strength, this feud became his providential meal, a grim lesson in how unresolved anger shapes destiny across births.

In their endless struggle, they forgot peace;
in Garuda's flight, their story found an end.

The episode marks a shift from mere appetite to purposeful action as Garuda advances toward his greater mission.
